Why PD

Personal Dedication.
It's not a tagline — it's the founding principle.

When the family started PD Towing of Houston, they named the company after the philosophy they ran it by: Personal Dedication. Going the extra mile for every community. Building relationships, not just clearing fire lanes.

Fifteen-plus years and countless tows later, every truck in the fleet still answers a property manager's call inside the same hour. Every vehicle moved is photographed from every angle, time-stamped, and called into the police line before the wrecker leaves the lot. Every storage facility we partner with is state-licensed, located within 20 minutes of the community, and accepts most major cards.

It's why apartment communities, strip malls, and HOAs from Cypress to Sugarland keep us on retainer. The work is the same as anyone else's. The way we do it isn't.

  • Curb & arrow markings
  • Tow-zone signs installed to code
